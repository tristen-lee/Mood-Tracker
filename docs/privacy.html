Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who this is for

Cairn is a mood tracking app built for people managing bipolar disorder. It is intended for adults 18 and older. If you are under 18, please do not use this app.

What we collect

When you create an account we store your first name and email address. Every time you check in, we store the data you enter — your mood score, sleep hours, energy level, symptoms, medications taken, and any notes you write. That's it. We don't collect your location, contacts, camera, or anything else on your device.

How it's stored

Your data lives in a secured PostgreSQL database. Your password is hashed with bcrypt — we cannot see it, and it is never stored in plain text. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Who can see your data

You. That's the only person who can access your check-in history. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, hand it to researchers, or use it to train AI models. Your health data stays yours.

Jasper and the AI

Jasper is an AI companion powered by the Claude API (Anthropic). When you start a conversation with Jasper, your recent check-in history is sent to Anthropic's API so Jasper can respond with context about how you've actually been doing. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how they handle that data — they do not use API data to train their models. You can read their policy at anthropic.com.

If you don't want your check-in data sent to any third party, don't use Jasper. Everything else in the app works without it.

Google Analytics

Cairn uses Google Analytics to track page views and general usage patterns — things like which pages are visited and how often. Your health data is never included. This helps understand how the app is used so it can be improved. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using a browser extension like uBlock Origin.

Push notifications

If you enable notifications, your browser handles the delivery through its push service (Google for Chrome, Mozilla for Firefox, Apple for Safari). We send the notification — your device's push service delivers it. No personal health data is included in notification payloads.

Your rights

You can export all your check-in data as a CSV from the Entries page at any time. You can delete your entire account and all associated data from the Settings page — this is permanent and cannot be undone. You can also delete individual entries from the Entries page.

Data retention

Your data is kept as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, everything is deleted immediately — entries, medications, account info, all of it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll update the date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
