What is Cairn?

Cairn is a daily mood tracker built for bipolar disorder. You log how you're doing each day and it shows you patterns over time. After enough data it'll start flagging when things look like they're heading somewhere — not a diagnosis, just a heads up from your own numbers.

The check-in

It's a short 6-step flow, one question at a time. Takes about a minute.

Mood and energy are 1–10 sliders. Sleep asks how many hours and whether you woke up feeling rested — that second part matters more than people expect. Feeling rested on low sleep is a known early sign of hypomania, so Cairn tracks it separately.

The symptom steps are just tap-what-applies. Elevated stuff on one screen, low stuff on the other. Nothing is required — if nothing applies, just skip through. The last step has medications (if you've added them in Settings) and a notes field if you want to write anything down.

Mood states

After each check-in Cairn assigns a mood state. Mania and depression are scored separately — they don't cancel each other out, which is how mixed states get caught.

Stable means nothing notable. Hypomanic is elevated but not fully manic. Manic is when the signals are strong enough that it's worth calling your care team. Depressed is what it sounds like. Mixed is both elevated and low at the same time — usually the roughest one. Severe is for when psychosis is showing up alongside depression.

Episode warnings

Once you have a few weeks of data, Cairn watches for slow-building patterns — sleep creeping down, scores trending, symptoms showing up repeatedly. If it picks something up, a card shows on your dashboard. Dismiss it if you want, it'll come back tomorrow if the pattern is still there.

Jasper

Jasper is an AI you can talk to inside the app. Before every conversation he reads your recent check-in history so he already knows what's been going on. Not a therapist, but he's actually paying attention to your data, not just giving generic responses.

Your data

Your data is private and only you can see it. You can export everything as a CSV from the Entries page and bring it to your doctor or therapist.
